Natasha

NATASHA ADLER. With a passion for making the world a better place, Natasha adds the art of discovery and innovation to NASH. She uses a critical eye and out of the box thinking to encourage a team full of innovators to design further, better and with more intention. She studied at Fanshawe College in the program of Interactive Media Design where she fell in love with design and user experience. She lives each day with a curious mind, looking for ways design, user experience and interactivity can become better. In doing all this she brings kindness to others and encourages others to do the same. Her mantra she lives by is “Throw Kindness Like Confetti”.
