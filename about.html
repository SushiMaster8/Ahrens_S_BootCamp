Natasha Adler
With a passion for making the world a better place, Natasha adds the art of discovery and innovation to NASH. She uses a critical eye and out of the box thinking to encourage a team full of innovators to design further, better and with more intention. She studied at Fanshawe College in the program of Interactive Media Design where she fell in love with design and user experience. She lives each day with a curious mind, looking for ways design, user experience and interactivity can become better. In doing all this she brings kindness to others and encourages others to do the same. Her mantra she lives by is “Throw Kindness Like Confetti”

Sasha Ahrens
Sasha Ahrens is a designer first, and brings his creativity to work every day. His experience with digital graphics, photography, web design, and videography he brings many talents to NASH. He is born and raised in London Ontario and is excited to be working where he grew up, and more excited to elevate creations at NASH.
His hobbies include drawing, running, music, hockey, playing video games, and reading.

HAYDEN BURNELL.
A small town guy with big city dreams, Hayden adds a blend of creativity and leadership to NASH Designs. Not people that nod their head and act like they agree".
